About the website


Concerning the strong summer forest fires in California, it would be helpful to launch an application that can predict the locations that are most likely to burn. The designed application can detect the likelihood of wild fire for different locations by machine learning algorithms. It can be used by environmentalists to protect the environment. Furthermore, it can be utilized by the people who inhabit such locations. They need to be on alert when the likelihood of fire is high. Jurisdictions such as cities and counties along with agencies such as Cal Fire and the US Forest Service can be notified of dangerous conditions. Moreover, It may be possible to programmatically recommend forestry practices to address the area-specific conditions that contribute to the likelihood of fire.
